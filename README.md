Spinny Revenue Growth Through Demand and Pricing Optimisation
Project Overview

Spinny is an online used-car retailing platform in India that focuses on bringing transparency, trust, and convenience to the pre-owned car market. Operating across 36 car hubs in 15 cities, Spinny manages the entire lifecycle of used-car transactions using technology-driven processes.

This project analyzes large-scale used-car transaction data to identify demand patterns, revenue drivers, and pricing opportunities with the objective of increasing completed transactions and overall revenue.

Problem Statement

Spinny aims to increase revenue by improving transaction completion rates. Achieving this requires:

Understanding regional demand patterns

Identifying high-demand car models and attributes

Optimizing pricing strategies based on demand and car characteristics

Minimizing unsold inventory while maximizing profitability
